About Us

SSA is an inventive, flexible and quality conscious sourcing agency for products such as Bed Linen, Bedspread, Cushions, Bathmats, Doormats, Rugs, Curtains, Shower Curtains and various other products.From product sourcing to factory sourcing, production planning and management, quality assurance (coming soon) all the aspects are handled by us at a minimal cost depending on the volume of business. SSA is engaged in offering single point solution for buyers by providing them a trustworthy platform and fulfilling their requirements.

Our Values

QUALITY ASSUARANCE

Clients merchandise is made as per the highest quality standard in an ethical work environment.

TRANSPARENCY

We encourage our clients to visit the factories of supplier with whom we work on their behalf. Trust, honesty, transparency are the building blocks of a positive reputation.

SOCIAL COMPLIANCE

All our suppliers are in complete compliance with a very strict and precise social, labour and environmental standards.

ACCOUNTABILITY

Being responsible is the first step. 100% accountability for all actions and making it a point to achieve all commitments smoothly helps in instilling belief in the work we do.
